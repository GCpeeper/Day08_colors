White Water

What is White Water

White water or rapids are a place where a river narrows or constricts. This causes complex currents and large waves. There are many sports that utilizes these rivers and white water. There are many features in white water. There are holes, where a large rock cuases water to go up and over and to slow down. There are pour overs, this is where a rock is just covered by water, similar to a holes however they can be more dangerous becuase your vessel can get stuck on one. There are also, wave trains, generally at the end of a rapid it is where the water is pushed to the center of the river and it creates a fun rollercoster like experience. If there is only water getting pushed off one side of the river, this creates lateral wave, waves that aren't going in the direction of the current

What sports use white water

Some examples are White Water Rafting, White Water SUPing(stand up paddleboarding). Many of these sports are considered extreme sports, however much of that is based on the river and the rapid. There are a lot of different rivers, in the world each has a different style of white water, becuase of how it formed. This allows for many rivers to not be extreme: Making a river for almost everybody
